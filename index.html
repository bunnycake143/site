About Me:

Hello! My name is Vy and welcome to Sakura Studios. I am currently a college student in North Carolina who loves to spend most of my free time being creative.
 
This website is an online portfolio to showcase and share my artisitc projects in categories of crocheting, embroidery, illustrations, perler beads, and collages. My central aesthetics are pastel and kawaii.
 
Outside of these innovative hobbies I also enjoy reading, biking, and volunteering.
 
Interesting Facts: Pandas are my favorite animals, I love Studio Ghibli Movies, and I hate tomatoes!
